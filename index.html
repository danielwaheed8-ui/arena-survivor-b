Meet your pediatrician

Care that knows your child by name — and by heart.

For more than 15 years, Dr. Rubina Azam has cared for newborns, children, and teens with a rare combination of deep clinical expertise and genuine warmth. She practices with a conservative, preventive mindset — pairing modern, evidence‑based medicine with an integrative, whole‑child approach that seeks the root cause, not just the symptom.

Dr. Azam founded Mango Pediatrics to practice medicine the way she always believed it should be: unhurried, personal, and built around real relationships with the families she serves.

  • Residency — Pediatrics & Adolescent MedicineHoward University Hospital (Washington, DC) & Austin Children’s Hospital (Austin, TX)
  • Advanced fellowship — Neonatal Intensive Care (NICU)The Hospital for Sick Children, Toronto
  • Fellowship‑trained in Integrative MedicineUniversity of Arizona — program founded by Dr. Andrew Weil
  • Diplomate, American Board of Integrative & Holistic MedicineSince 2013
